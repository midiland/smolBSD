About

smolBSD is a meta-operating system built on top of NetBSD. It lets you compose your own UNIX environment ‚Äî from a single-purpose microservice system to a fully-custom OS image ‚Äî in just a few minutes.

The smolBSD environment uses the netbsd-MICROVM kernel as its foundation, leveraging the same portable, reliable codebase that powers NetBSD itself. You decide what to include ‚Äî sshd, httpd, or your own service ‚Äî and smolBSD builds a coherent, minimal, bootable image ready to run anywhere.
